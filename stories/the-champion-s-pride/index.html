The Champion's Pride

Preamble

In the muffled silence of a nocturnal Shinkansen, Takeshi, the undisputed champion of ōgi, accepts a mysterious stranger's proposal for a friendly game. Draped in a kimono with cerulean reflections, she deploys a game as fascinating as it is unusual, initiating a confrontation that quickly transcends the traditional boundaries of the board.

As the train cuts through the darkness towards Tokyo, what was meant to be just a distraction transforms into a trial where the champion's pride becomes his most formidable adversary. On this night when certainties waver, Takeshi is yet unaware that this chance encounter will forever mark his existence.

Between the Pages

The Apparition

The sliding of the carriage door broke Takeshi's train of thought. A silhouette appeared in the doorway, draped in a deep blue kimono that seemed to absorb the carriage's dim light. The traditional garment, unusual in a Shinkansen, drew attention without demanding it — like a masterpiece positioned strategically on a game board.

The First Vertigo

Time now seemed to flow with an insidious slowness, like honey frozen by the cold. The pieces on the board, once familiar to Takeshi, took on strangely moving contours under his exhausted gaze. His mind, despite the fatigue seeping into his thoughts, continued to calculate, anticipate, weaving increasingly complex strategies.
